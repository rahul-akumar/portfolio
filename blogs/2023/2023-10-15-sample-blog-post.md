# Welcome to My Blog!

This is a sample blog post to demonstrate how to structure your Markdown files. The frontmatter (the section between the `---` markers at the top) contains metadata about your post:

- **title**: The title of your blog post (required)
- **subtitle**: A short description or summary (optional)
- **date**: Publication date in YYYY-MM-DD format (required)
- **keywords**: Array of relevant tags/keywords (optional)
